    ABOUT US

    Test R&D

    Responsible for the design, fabrication, assembly and
    evaluation of all IC testing equipment including schematic
    diagram, test board, test probe, test socket, PCB and test
    program. Coordinates with RK Test Development and Design
    with each project / device.

    Test Probe Department

    Responsible for the fabrication of Test Probe products
    used in EDS-Wafer Testing, covering the primary functions
    of design and production.

    PCB Design Department

    Responsible for the development of PCB for LSI Test box.

    Test Box Department

    Responsible for the assembly of test equipment hardware
    and accessories used for wafer testing and final test processes.

    Test Solution Department 1 & 2

    Responsible for the development of software and prototype
    hardware to be used in testing of wafer and LSI finished products.

    Test Solution Department Support

    Responsible for the quality improvement in Test Development
    process, including the education of Test Development
    Engineers and standardization of Test Development Process.

    Project Control Department

    Responsible for the overall order-in and job-out of each project,
    including 4M preparations and material inventory control.
